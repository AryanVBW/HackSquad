AgriConnect

Empowering Farmers with AI-Driven Solutions

About AgriConnect

AgriConnect is an innovative platform designed to solve key challenges faced by Indian farmers. By combining direct market access, price prediction, and crop disease management, we empower farmers with the tools they need for success.

Features

Direct Market Access

Connects farmers directly with consumers and retailers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices for their produce.

Price Prediction

AI-ML models predict the prices of agri-horticultural commodities like pulses and vegetables, helping farmers make informed decisions.

Crop Disease Management

AI-driven system analyzes crop images and environmental data to predict potential diseases and provide actionable insights.

Technologies Used

  • Programming Languages: Python, Java, Swift
  • Frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, React Native
  • APIs: OpenAI, Weather APIs, Agricultural Databases
  • Cloud Services: AWS, Google Cloud

Impact & Benefits

AgriConnect is designed to create significant social, economic, and environmental impact:

  • Increases farmers' income by eliminating middlemen.
  • Promotes sustainable farming practices.
  • Improves crop health management and reduces losses.
